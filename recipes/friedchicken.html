These crispy fried chicken tenders are the ultimate comfort food‚Äîtender and juicy on the inside, with a perfectly seasoned, crunchy exterior. Served on a bed of fresh lettuce with a classic creamy dip, they are perfect as an appetizer, a party snack, or a main course. The simple breading ensures maximum flavor and crunch.

Ingredients List

  • 500g chicken breast, cut into strips (tenders)
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs (panko recommended for extra crispiness)
  • 2 large eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper (optional)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Vegetable oil, for frying
  • Fresh lettuce leaves
  • Creamy dip (mayonnaise, aioli, or ranch)

Steps to Make

  1. Prepare the Chicken: Pat the chicken tenders dry with paper towels. Season them generously with salt and pepper.
  2. Set up the Breading Station: Arrange three separate shallow dishes: flour mixture, egg wash, and breadcrumbs.
  3. Bread the Tenders: Dredge the tender in the flour mixture, then the egg wash, and finally coat it completely in the breadcrumbs, pressing firmly.
  4. Heat the Oil: Fill a pan with about 1 inch of oil. Heat the oil to 350¬∞F (175¬∞C).
  5. Fry the Chicken: Carefully place the breaded tenders into the hot oil. Fry for 3-4 minutes per side until golden brown and cooked through.
  6. Drain and Serve: Remove the tenders and place them on a wire rack or paper towels to drain excess oil.
  7. Assemble: Arrange a platter with fresh lettuce leaves, pile the crispy chicken tenders, and serve with your creamy dip.
